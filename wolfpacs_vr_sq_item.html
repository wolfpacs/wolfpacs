Description

Sequence Item

PITFALL: I found out this the hard way. The delination tags should not be read as a 32bit tag, but as a normal (gggg, eeee) tag. Example, FFFE:16, E0DD:16 not FFFEE0DD:32

PITFALL: There can be more than one element in the sequence. Example:

(0070,0009) SQ (Sequence with explicit length #=31) # 3092, 1 GraphicObjectSequence (fffe,e000) na (Item with explicit length #=6) # 84, 1 Item (0070,0005) CS [PIXEL] # 6, 1 GraphicAnnotationUnits (0070,0020) US 2 # 2, 1 GraphicDimensions (0070,0021) US 2 # 2, 1 NumberOfGraphicPoints (0070,0022) FL 128\21\353\21 # 16, 4 GraphicData (0070,0023) CS [POLYLINE] # 8, 1 GraphicType (0070,0024) CS [N] # 2, 1 GraphicFilled (fffe,e00d) na (ItemDelimitationItem for re-encoding) # 0, 0 ItemDelimitationItem (fffe,e000) na (Item with explicit length #=6) # 84, 1 Item (0070,0005) CS [PIXEL] # 6, 1 GraphicAnnotationUnits (0070,0020) US 2 # 2, 1 GraphicDimensions (0070,0021) US 2 # 2, 1 NumberOfGraphicPoints (0070,0022) FL 128\18\128\24 # 16, 4 GraphicData (0070,0023) CS [POLYLINE] # 8, 1 GraphicType (0070,0024) CS [N] # 2, 1 GraphicFilled (fffe,e00d) na (ItemDelimitationItem for re-encoding) # 0, 0 ItemDelimitationItem ...
